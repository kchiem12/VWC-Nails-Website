About us

Our Story

Since 2008, our family-owned business has only grown throughout the years. We have trained and worked diligently in providing our clients with nail care service that transcends expectations, with most of our technicians having more than 7 years of experience. We strive not only in maintaining our professionalism, but also in cultivating a welcoming environment. We want clients to feel relaxed and allow them to chat with our technicians as if they were talking to a close friend.

We only use top-of-the-line products, such as OPI, Essi, D&D, and many more for our clients. Of our nail polishes, we have a wide variety of colors to choose from. If we do not have a color or brand of nail polish that you are looking for on our shelves, then you are more than welcome to bring your own nail polish! However, we are not responsible for the performance of the nail polish you bring in.
